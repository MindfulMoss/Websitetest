Who we are?

Astro Research Lab is a pioneering satellite manufacturer revolutionizing the aerospace landscape in South East Asia. With a commitment to cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions, they push the boundaries of satellite design, manufacturing, and deployment.

Provide advanced Space Systems, including AIS Payload, RGB Cameras, On-Board Computers (OBC), Electrical Power Systems (EPS), and robust satellite structures.
